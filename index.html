About FMT

FMT Trust is a community-driven charitable initiative committed to uplifting economically weaker members of our community through compassion, dignity, and sustainable support. We believe that charity should not only provide immediate relief but also empower individuals and families to build a better future.

Our mission is to help people become self-reliant by supporting initiatives that create lasting positive impact. This includes assisting deserving students with educational expenses, providing financial support to widows for their essential monthly needs, and offering interest-free (Qard Hasan) financial assistance to individuals seeking to establish or grow small businesses and improve their livelihoods.

To ensure transparency and accountability, FMT combines the spirit of charitable giving with modern digital solutions, enabling members to securely access donation records and community services while maintaining the highest standards of trust.

At FMT, every contribution represents hope, every act of kindness strengthens our community, and every life transformed brings us closer to our vision of a more compassionate and self-sustaining society.
